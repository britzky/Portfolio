About me

As a Fullstack Software Engineer with a strong passion for front-end development, I thrive on creating intuitive, visually appealing, and highly functional user experiences. I have experience working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, modern frameworks and libraries like React, Material UI, Bootstrap, as well as some back-end experience with Python and Flask. I recently completed a 10-week full-stack software development program, where I built several personal projects and contributed to group projects with other members of my cohort, continually refining my skills and staying updated with the latest industry trends. My strong problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, and dedication to learning make me a perfect fit for any team looking for a front-end developer. In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on being a strong communicator, adaptable team player, and quick learner. I enjoy keeping the mood light while working hard, which helps maintain a positive and collaborative atmosphere among my colleagues. I'm eager to collaborate with other developers, designers, and stakeholders to create outstanding web applications and contribute to the success of the team. If you're looking for a motivated, ambitious and fun front-end developer who's always ready for a challenge, I'd love to discuss how I can contribute to your company's projects. Please feel free to browse my portfolio, check out my GitHub, and get in touch with me through my contact information below. I look forward to the opportunity to work together!

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy exploring the outdoors with my dog, playing video games and board games, cooking healthy meals to fuel my body and mind, and playing disc golf to stay active and socialize with friends. These activities help me maintain a balanced lifestyle, recharge my creativity, and stay inspired to tackle new challenges in my work as a front-end developer.
